About Fastrux

Two decades of moving what matters — reliably, efficiently, and with care.

Moving the world's cargo since 2004

Fastrux was founded with a single mission: make global freight simple. What started as a small domestic trucking operation in New York has grown into a full-service international logistics company serving clients in over 180 countries.

We believe every shipment tells a story — a manufacturer reaching new markets, a family receiving goods from home, a startup scaling globally. We treat each one with the same urgency and care.

Today our team of 3,400+ logistics professionals operate across 6 continents, supported by a proprietary technology platform that gives customers real-time visibility at every step.

Our Journey

From a single truck in New York to a global logistics powerhouse.

2004
Founded in New York
Marcus Reynolds launches Fastrux with 3 trucks and a vision to simplify domestic freight.
2008
First International Route
Expanded into trans-Atlantic ocean freight, partnering with major European shipping lines.
2013
Air Freight Division Launched
Introduced express air freight for time-critical cargo, growing to 40 airline partnerships in year one.
2018
Fastrux Technology Platform
Launched proprietary real-time tracking and supply chain visibility platform used by 10,000+ shippers.
2022
Carbon-Neutral Pledge
Committed to net-zero emissions by 2035. Introduced carbon offset options for all shipment types.
2026

Reliable Ocean Freight for Any Volume, Any Destination

Whether you're moving a single container or a full fleet of vessels, Fastrux's ocean freight division delivers cost-efficient, dependable maritime transportation on every major trade lane — Asia-Pacific, Trans-Atlantic, Trans-Pacific, and beyond.

We offer Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than-Container Load (LCL) options, giving businesses of all sizes access to the world's most economical long-haul freight mode. Our dedicated ocean freight managers handle every step from booking to port clearance.

What's Included

Every Fastrux ocean freight booking comes with a full suite of services as standard.

FCL & LCL Options

Full Container Load for high-volume shipments, or consolidate with other cargo in a Less-than-Container Load to save on smaller consignments.

Real-Time Vessel Tracking

Monitor your container's exact location 24/7 using our live tracking dashboard with AIS vessel data updated every 15 minutes.

Customs & Documentation

Our licensed customs brokers handle bills of lading, certificates of origin, import/export declarations, and all port paperwork.

Cargo Insurance

Comprehensive marine cargo insurance with competitive rates. Coverage from port of origin through to final delivery destination.

Reefer & Hazmat

Refrigerated containers for temperature-sensitive cargo, plus specialist handling for hazardous materials in full regulatory compliance.

Dedicated Account Manager

One point of contact who knows your business, your cargo, and your lanes — available by phone, email, or live chat.
